Modern information technology plays a crucial role in everyday human life. Computers, smartphones, and digital systems are present in almost every field, including education, healthcare, and entertainment. The development of the internet has made access to information fast and widely available, significantly changing the way people communicate and work. However, this progress also brings challenges such as data security, privacy protection, and the growing dependence on digital devices. Responsible use of technology requires not only technical skills but also awareness of its social impact.
